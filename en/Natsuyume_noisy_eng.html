NATSUME NOISY

When the most beautiful moments would have deserved to be frozen in time.

official clip of natsume noisy music

Natsume noisy is a J-pop music released on 10/08/2022, performed by singer Asaka and created and written by shikura chiyomaru.

This music was made to serve as a second theme song for the series time shadow along with Voice and dreamer also made by the same duo to serve as background music, time shadow being a series with drama/mystery/romance/supernatural genres and crime/family/time travel themes.

But who are asaka and shikura chiyomaru?

Photograph of the singer Asaka

Asaka is a 23 years old Japanese pop singer, chorus and lyricist, belonging to the group "Okunai Katsudou Circle", and passionate about cosplay and anime, she has produced 2 albums "HEART TOUCH" and "19BOX" but also 10 other musics.

Photograph of composer shikura chiyomaru

Shikura Chiyomaru is a 52 year old Japanese Author, Composer, Musician, Lyricist, part of the MAGES agency and the DystopiaGround group. He is mainly known for having directed several credits and ost for the steins gate series.

Is it accessible?

Yes for all hearing people.

Marketing

Nowadays, a lot of music bands like J Pop promote their albums by making theme music for animation studios, in the case of our music, it was made as one of the theme music for the anime adaptation of Time Shadow, unfortunately, from a worldwide point of view, this choice was a big mistake as the series was only broadcasted in Japan.

Conclusion

Natsume noisy will be able to please everyone but only time shadow fans can really be attracted by the music because it was not created by a music group but only by a duo, giving it as only possibility of affect, its link with this series.

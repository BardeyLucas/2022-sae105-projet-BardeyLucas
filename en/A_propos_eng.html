About tengu road

Tengu road is a travel/mapping site (infrastructure database, mapping of Japan) for fans of East Asia. It is accessible free of charge to any Internet user with an Internet access and it is possible to create a member account, also free of charge, to take advantage of advanced features (to report unsuitable infrastructure, to write reviews, to post comments and to file reports).

Tengu-road.fr is hosted by Infomaniak whose head office is located at Les Acacias in the town of Carrouge in the canton of Geneva in Switzerland.

To contact us, go to this form .

Personal data

The personal data collected by Tengu-road.fr in the case of the creation of a member account or a guest account since the beta test version* of the site or in the future versions are in no case communicated to third parties, the passwords are encrypted and it is impossible to recover it if it is forgotten, it will be necessary to make a request of loss of password in this case.

(The beta test features are currently only accessible by the moderation team and other employees working on the tengu-road.fr website).

Tengu-road.com stores the user's IP address when he/she connects to the site, posts a comment, review or report on the site.

On Tengu-road.fr, the user has the right to access, rectify and delete data concerning him/her. Indeed, at any time, it is possible for a member to modify his comments, messages on the forum and to delete his account.

Les cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files deposited on the hard disk of the Internet user, during the consultation of certain websites, and which keep information for a later connection.

What are they used for?

  • -Firstly, identification cookies allow you to be identified as soon as you arrive on our site in order to automatically connect you to your user profile.
  • -Analytical cookies allow us to produce statistics on the number of visitors, the use of our site (section and content visited, itinerary, etc.) and the popularity of the places listed on our map. They will help us to better understand our users in order to improve and optimize our site for you.
  • -Your positioning cookies will be used to give your position on the cartography part of the site and therefore to find you more easily when you travel.

Origin of the project

The tengu road website was created for the MMi1 2022-23 individual project organised by the MMI department of the IUT Belfort Montbéliard.

The idea started with the research of the general theme of the site, and the answer was quickly found, Japan and its culture was the theme which polarized me the most. And it is while looking for a theme in connection that the idea came to me.

I wanted to create a travel and geolocation website and what better idea to get out of the game than by trying to reach a public often left out, but also their relatives.

That's why I created a geolocation site that is even more accessible than the others, a site that really indicates the lack of accessibility of the places presented to inform people with a temporary or permanent disability, firstly by talking directly about the different risks of each place in their respective articles/leaflets, but also in a more individual way by indicating directly to the account holder whether or not the place will be accessible to him/her, thanks to a small icon that appears in the corner of the articles, leaflets and thumbnails that are linked to them.
